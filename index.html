Features

What SFT Does

🔍

Auto-Detection

Detects Samsung devices via ADB, Fastboot, Heimdall PIT, and USB descriptors. Reads model, serial, firmware, CSC, chipset, and battery.

🔧

Firmware Decoder

Parses 13-character Samsung firmware strings into hardware, market, update, and build segments. Instant identification.

🛡️

Rollback Prevention

Hardware mismatch, chipset mismatch, and bootloader version checks. Prevents bricking from incompatible firmware.

🌍

CSC Database

237+ Country Specific Codes with carrier, region, Samsung Pay, VoLTE, and 5G support. Includes 25 South African codes.

📱

Flash Guide

Step-by-step Odin flashing instructions with model-specific guidance. CSC vs HOME_CSC, pre-flash checklist, post-flash verification.

✅

Firmware Validation

tar.md5 integrity verification, content listing, MD5 checksum. Ensures firmware files are not corrupted before flashing.
