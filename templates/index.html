Reset Types Explained

Choose the right operation for your situation.

🔑

MAC Address Override

Resets the hardware MAC address to factory default or applies a custom override. Reversible.
🔄

Factory Reset via USB

Performs a full wipe and reinstall of the stock Samsung firmware without touching the bootloader.
📤

Firmware Removal

Remove, replace, or downgrade firmware. Includes emergency recovery and bloatware removal.

Safety Notice: Always back up your data before proceeding. This tool performs irreversible operations. Ensure your device has at least 30% battery before starting.
